Current-partners Pinyindiit Foundation collaborates with several organizations in its efforts to improve education for young people in South Sudan. These partnerships provide the foundation with the necessary resources and expertise to achieve its mission of empowering underserved students through education. Through these collaborations, Pinyindiit Foundation works closely with organizations that support refugees in the country, as well as those that focus on education and community development in South Sudan. Together, these partnerships enable Pinyindiit Foundation to identify and support students in need and provide them with school stationery and scholarships.

Active Members Pinydiit Foundation's active members are a dedicated group of volunteers who are committed to the cause and share the belief that education is a fundamental right that should be accessible to all. The active members of the Pinyindiit Foundation are composed of individuals from diverse backgrounds, including educators, community leaders, and individuals with a passion for social justice. They work tirelessly to identify and support students who face financial and social barriers to education. Through their efforts, the foundation will be able to provide essential school supplies, such as books, pens, and backpacks, to students in need.

Who We Are

PINYDIIT FOUNDATION (PF) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization founded in January 2023 with the mission of providing school stationery and scholarships to underserved students of internally displaced persons (IDPs) and refugee communities of South Sudanese origin within South Sudan and other East African countries.

Mission

In developing countries, wars disrupt youths' education, affecting future generations. This problem is even more daunting in a new country such as South Sudan. Sudanese (South) children are disadvantaged by the wars and internal conflicts that hide their access to primary education. Forced to live in displaced and refugee camps, children of South Sudan need/lack many items required for proper education, such as school supplies. PINYDIIT FOUNDATION's mission is to provide school stationaries and scholarships to underserved students of internally displaced persons (IDPs) and refugee communities of South Sudan within South Sudan and other East African countries. In the developed world (e.g., USA), school supplies such as pencils, pens, and exercise books are taken for guarantee. However, in the mentioned-above places (new and wars-torn countries), these items are rare commodities that impair children's schooling and learning and disadvantage the next generations of society.

Vision

Our vision is to fill the gap for underserved and disadvantaged students of IDPs and refugee communities by supplying essential school items that facilitate their schooling and toward building a better society (whole society).
